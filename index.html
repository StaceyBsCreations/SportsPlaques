How It Works

1
Choose Product + Sport
Choose large plaque or small plaque, then pick your sport and template.
2
Add Athlete Details
Name, number, school/team, year, plus optional player details and stats.
3
Approve the Proof + 50% Deposit
We confirm pricing, send a proof, and send a Square invoice for a 50% deposit.
4
Finish + Final Payment
When finished and approved, we send the final 50% invoice before pickup or shipping.
Payment Policy
  • 50% deposit is required to begin production after your proof is approved.
  • Final 50% is due when the piece is finished and agreed to (before pickup/shipping).
  • Final pricing depends upon layers, customization, and shipping (if chosen) — we confirm final price before invoicing.
